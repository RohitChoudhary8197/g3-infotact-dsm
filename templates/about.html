Advanced Engine Health Monitoring

An AI-powered predictive maintenance platform designed to monitor bike engine performance, detect potential issues, and predict failures before they happen, helping users reduce downtime and maintenance costs.

Project Overview

Predictive Maintenance This project introduces a revolutionary approach to industrial equipment maintenance by leveraging Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. It continuously monitors critical bike engine parameters and provides early failure predictions, enabling proactive maintenance, reducing downtime, and improving overall operational efficiency. 🚀🔧🤖

By leveraging advanced Machine Learning algorithms, the system can accurately predict potential engine failures before they occur, helping reduce costly downtime, minimize unexpected breakdowns, and prevent expensive emergency repairs. 🚀🔧📊
